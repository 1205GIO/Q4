Documentation:

This app uses a trained Random Forest Classifier model to predict the likelihood of heart disease based on patient details.

The model was trained on a dataset of patient details and heart disease outcomes.

The app uses Streamlit to create a user-friendly interface for inputting patient details and displaying the prediction.
